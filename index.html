1. Olanka Sales Environment Introduction

Before we jump into the Olanka Sales Process, let’s look at a few basic terms to understand what we do as a business.

a. What is a business?

A business is all about buying and selling. We will be buying from our suppliers and selling it to customers.

b. So what do we do as a business at Olanka?

We buy from our travel-related global suppliers and sell it to global customers around the world.

c. Who are Olanka Customers?

Any person who wishes to buy travel packages, transport services, air tickets, visa services, or any kind of services related to their traveling, we call them as a client Customer.

d. There are 2 types of Olanka customers

  1. i. B2B (Business to Business) customer

    Travel agents & Tour Operators all over the world.

  2. ii. B2C (Business to Consumer) customer

    Direct travelers all over the world.

e. Who are Olanka Suppliers?

All kinds of service providers all over the world which are related to the travel business we call them as a supplier.

Olanka main global suppliers are:

  1. Hotels & resorts
  2. DMCs (Destination management companies)
  3. Transport providers
  4. Drivers & guides
  5. Tourist activity / excursion providers
  6. Visa & immigration services
  7. Airlines & airline consolidated agents
  8. Travel Insurances companies

f. What are Olanka products?

At Olanka, we have 3 types of travel service products:

  1. Tailor-made travel arrangements (curated travel)
  2. Fixed Tour Packages
  3. Small Groups (group tours/fix departure tours)

These 3 types of different products can be from any of the destinations we operate.

g. Currently we operate to below destinations

  1. Sri Lanka
  2. Maldives
  3. Vietnam
  4. Indonesia
  5. Dubai

f. Inquiry Referral & My Leads

A referral happens when your existing customer or a lead passes you some other potential customer contacts, and you add them to the OTAS. "My Lead" refers to an inquiry when you find a potential customer by yourself without Olanka marketing support. The main difference between "My Leads" and "Referrals" is that referrals are referred by existing customers in OTAS, while "My Leads" are not.

g. Inquiry Re Allocation

If an inquiry has been allocated to someone mistakenly or if the sales process is violated by a tour consultant, an inquiry re-allocation can take place. In a re-allocation, you will be notified clearly by the sales operations department via WhatsApp or phone call.

Sales Process KPI

You're required to get in touch with the newly allocated customer within 10 minutes via Twilio Frontline App, either through a call or WhatsApp Business chat.

  • If you are unable to meet this timeline, you need to update your sales notes in OTAS with valid reasons.
  • If you are unable to handle this customer at the current time, you need to contact the sales operations team and re-allocate this inquiry to another tour consultant.

Sales Audit Factor

From the time the new inquiry is allocated to you, OTAS will start recording timers. OTAS will send notification emails if you fail to follow the steps. These notifications are CCed to your managers and are also tied to your performance reviews.

You will be issued a fine notice from OTAS and your sales incentives may be deducted accordingly if you fail to follow the process.

h. Be the first!

At Olanka, we want to be the first company to get in touch with the customer to discuss their travel plans and provide the required travel quotes. Here's how we can achieve this:

  1. Customer first searches online for a tour provider. Olanka and a few other companies can appear in their search results.
  2. Customers typically visit multiple travel websites, engage with different companies, and compare quotes.
  3. With the help of Olanka's 24/7 web chat team, we can make an initial good impression on customers by engaging with them online and gathering their basic requirements.
  4. The key step lies in you, the "Tour Consultant," getting in touch with the customer quickly.
  5. At Olanka, the tour consultant must contact the customer within 10 minutes from the moment the customer engages with the Olanka Web Chat Team. The customer's initial communication can take place through web form submission or web chat.
  6. Regardless of the method they initiate the conversation, the OTAS timer starts recording. Your KPI as a tour consultant is to contact the customer in less than 10 minutes.
  7. Why within 10 minutes?
    1. The customer is still online, making it an opportune time to discuss the detailed requirements of their tour plan. They are actively searching for tour companies at this moment.
    2. The customer is in the mindset of planning their upcoming tour, providing a genuine need and motivation for conversation.
  8. In most cases, this will be the first time the customer encounters the "Olanka" brand name. Olanka is not yet globally recognized like Booking.com or Airbnb. Therefore, it is crucial to build “TRUST” in the brand name of Olanka by making a great first impression through the web chat team and the tour consultant. it is critical to build the “TRUST” an unknown brand name Olanka.

Customer Requirement Gathering

Customer Requirement Gathering (RG) step is one of the most important steps in our sales process. We have a lot of emphasis on this step, so please pay attention to details here. For your easier understanding of RG, we have broken it down to 3 sections. Before, During & After the RG call.

Do’s Don'ts
Prepare yourself & environment before the call.
  • Your internet connection must be good & steady.
  • You should NOT have background noises.
  • You should have a clear understanding of what questions you're going to ask. Prepare and call.
  • You must have a way to take down your notes
 DO NOT call if you have background noises. Eg if you're in the bus or outside the roads.
Good practise WhatsApp message first before calling Prepare yourself with questions and DO NOT call the customer for different questions at direct times. Ask all your questions in one call.
It’s OK to do the RG using WhatsApp text messaging if the client is comfortable. However it will be super great if you can voice call the customer if possible. It will lead to a greater “Trust” building. DO NOT repeat the same questions which customers already answered to the Web Chat Team. But you can clarify, instead.
